Question: What is an ezine?

Answer: An ezine is an Electronic MagaZINE -- thus called an E-ZINE. The DrawingFree Ezine is created to start (or further) your own art making enjoyment. It is designed to give you ideas and simple steps to work with to increase your ability and confidence to make your own art. You can make your own art using the guidance of the DrawingFree Ezine. The DrawingFree Ezine is FR*EE.

Question: Why is the DrawingFree Ezine called DrawingFree?

Answer: Drawing is at the root of all the visual the arts. And, as Janis Joplin belted out, "Nothing don't mean nothing honey if it ain't fr*ee". She didn't mean the cost. She meant that you've got to be fr*ee. DrawingFree works to teach you to draw freely without self-judgement or self-critisism.

How Margaret Welty, M.A. the creator of the DrawingFree Ezine came to create DrawingFree:

Close to twenty years ago I unknowingly embarked on a journey to learn how to teach people to teach themselves to draw. In the beginning I used what most teachers were using at the time and I was shocked at the results. It seemed that there was very little positive or immediate evidence of anyone truly learning to draw . It was very slow going. In desperation, I finally resorted to assigning a high school project of copying a photograph using tiny dots. Even that did not work with the majority of the students. Lots of drawing but little progress. Despite the methods, after about 15 weeks of drawing classes most of the students began to shift and make progress in their drawings. I was curious when magically everyone seemed to begin to thrive and their drawings progressed. That was long before the brain research methods that we have today began to prove what I suspected about learning to draw. All my classroom observation, experimentation and intuitive notions led me to believe that we have to create new neural pathways throughout our brain, eyes and hands in order to draw and think visually. Today I call this "creating the drawing brain". The drawing brain that we create through using the DrawingFree methods is composed of the brain constellations and connections that are essential for drawing well. As we practice all sorts of drawing by playing with art materials we begin to build our drawing brains. The DrawingFree Ezine is designed to give you ways to practice making art that will add up to the skills that you need to draw and see. DrawingFree is here to help you expand your artistic and creative self.
